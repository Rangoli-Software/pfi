Item Sizing

Please be aware that our garment sizes may not correspond directly to European or American sizes.

For each item we provide a sizing chart that specifies the measurements of important dimensions. Please check on our chart to make sure the sizing is correct before placing your order.

Wash Care

Tangail
  • Do Not Soak
  • Turn Inside-Out
  • Machine Wash Cold
  • Mild Detergent
  • Gentle Cycle
  • Do Not Bleach
  • Dry In Shade
  • Medium Iron

Tangail is a delicate fabric, but with the proper care, your Tangail items will maintain their look for a long time. Some of my clients have owned their Tangail ArtWear pieces (where the entire dress is made of Tangail) for over 10 years, and the garments still look good!

Availability

You might have noticed that many of our creations are either one-of-a-kind or limited edition. Since our garments are on sale in several physical locations in addition to the website, there is a small chance that the one-of-a-kind item that you have selected has already been sold. In this case, we will let you select another item of the same kind, or refund your purchase price, as you prefer.

A word about packaging

As much as anyone else, we love it when beautiful packages arrive in the mail. However, we are mindful that all of this extra packaging (used just once) is not at all eco-friendly. To lower our overall resource usage footprint, we recycle cardboard waste (all those Amazon boxes, anyone), and use low-cost, bio-degradable shipping envelopes, and bio-degradable cellophane envelopes for garment protection in transit.

We want you to value the experience of using our garments, and have reluctantly chosen to degrade your experience of receiving them. We hope you understand.

Shipping

India Post

Our standard world-wide shipping method, and the one that we recommend to most international customers, is India Post's registered letter / parcel. These packages reach you in 14-15 business days world-wide. Over the years we have sent hundreds of packages by India Post and despite an occasional (very rare) delayed delivery, nothing has ever been lost. These packages are trackable on the India Post website, but require a signature at the time of delivery.

Courier

If you are in a hurry, We can also send packages by courier. This generally costs 2-3 times as much as India Post for international shipping, but the package will reach just about anywhere in the world in 4-5 business days (exclusive of customs delays).

Duties & Tariffs for international shipping

Our prices include applicable Indian VAT, but do not include Customs and any other import duties or tariffs that might be levied in the destination country. In fact, we do not know what the precise duties are for every destination, and which articles / packages will require payment of duties, so we cannot calculate these in advance. If there are any additional duties, you will have to pay extra at the time you receive the package. Generally, the courier or delivery agent will contact you directly to pay for any extra charges, or charge you at the time of delivery.
